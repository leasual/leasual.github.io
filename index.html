02 — About

A tiny studio, three apps, a lot of tea.

Geekdroid is a small indie studio. We design, code, illustrate, and ship everything ourselves — which means every pixel, every sound, every tap has a reason to exist.

We grew up on old Macintosh interfaces and traditional Chinese ink painting. Somewhere between those two worlds we find the apps we want to make: small, opinionated, a little strange, hopefully good company.

If any of that resonates, say hi.
